Valentine’s Day Zoom virtual backgrounds by Canva

Valentine’s Day means different things to different people. To some, it’s a romantic occasion to celebrate with a partner. To others, it’s a nice time to get together with friends and family.

Wherever you fall on this spectrum, there’s always a love-themed online event that you can join. Channel the spirit of the occasion by putting up a Valentine Zoom background you’ve made from Canva’s free templates. From simple to ornate and heart-filled, you can easily come up with charming Zoom virtual backgrounds for any virtual Valentine’s get-together. Design backdrops for online dates, virtual wine nights, or Valentine’s events at work and school.

To find the perfect template, filter the search results for Valentine Zoom background designs by style and theme. Once something catches your fancy, begin customizing it by tweaking the color palette. You can switch the background color—maybe add some texture—as well as the colors in the featured graphics. Look through Canva’s media library for additional visual elements. Add a ribbon border, floral icons, heart-shaped illustrations, chocolate vectors, and more by dragging and dropping them onto the layout. Adjust their placements and resize as needed. If you’re adding photos, check out the available filters and effects that can enhance them further.

Let your virtual backgrounds for Zoom do the greeting on Valentine’s Day. Edit the sample message on your template or create a text box and type a new greeting. Explore the different font pairings and colors you can use, starting with Canva’s suggested typography. Remember to keep the text short and sweet, since it’s a background. Be mindful of your chosen font, too, so your message will be easy to read and pleasing to the eye.

Test how your design would look on camera. Save and download the file in JPG or PNG and upload it to your Zoom account. Canva also saves your design projects, so you can always return for Valentine’s Day next year.
